# UK CAA Regulations & Test Sites

## What You Need to Fly Legally

### Mandatory (Do This Week)

| Step | What | Cost | Time |
|------|------|------|------|
| 1 | **Flyer ID** — online test at register-drones.caa.co.uk | Free | 30 min |
| 2 | **Operator ID** — same portal | £11.79/yr | 10 min |
| 3 | **BMFA Membership** — insurance + club access | ~£40/yr | 1 week |
| 4 | **Join a club** — ERFC or Caterham | Varies | Contact them |

### Your Drone's Classification

- MTOW 5-15 kg → **Open Category A3** ("far from people") for VLOS flights
- Too heavy for A2 (4kg limit)
- Privately built = "legacy" aircraft (no UK class mark needed)
- **Remote ID** mandatory by January 2028

### Open A3 Rules

| Rule | Limit |
|------|-------|
| Max altitude | 120m (400ft) AGL |
| Distance from people | 50m minimum |
| Distance from buildings | 50m minimum |
| Distance from residential/commercial areas | 150m minimum |
| Line of sight | Mandatory at all times |
| Airport FRZs | Strictly prohibited |

### Autonomous/Waypoint Flights

- Waypoint following under VLOS is fine under Open A3
- **Must maintain visual contact and ability to take manual control at any time**
- RC transmitter with kill-switch is required as safety override
- **BVLOS requires Specific Category Operational Authorisation** (£500-2,185+, UK SORA risk assessment)

### Insurance

- BMFA membership includes £5M public liability
- Not legally required for non-commercial Open Category, but strongly recommended
- Commercial operations require minimum ~£780,000 third-party coverage

## Test Sites

### ❌ Epsom Downs — NOT Suitable

- Public open space with walkers, horse riders
- Racehorse training area — drone could spook horses
- Cannot satisfy 150m from recreational areas requirement
- Not in airport FRZ (23km from Heathrow, 18km from Gatwick) but under London LTMA — check altitude restrictions

### ✅ Recommended Sites

**Epsom Radio Flying Club (ERFC)**
- Fixed-wing up to 20kg ✓
- 75m × 12m grass strip
- Flying days: Wed, Thu, Sun
- Website: erfc.bmfa.club
- BMFA affiliated

**Caterham & District Model Flying Club**
- 7 days/week access
- Mainly fixed wing
- Near South Godstone, Surrey
- Website: cdmfc.org.uk

**BMFA Club Finder**
- bmfa.justgo.com/clubFinder.html
- 750+ clubs, 1000+ sites nationwide

### Pre-Flight Checklist (Every Flight)

- [ ] Check airspace on NATS / Drone Assist app
- [ ] Check NOTAMs for temporary restrictions
- [ ] Confirm weather within operating envelope
- [ ] Operator ID displayed on aircraft
- [ ] Carry Flyer ID proof
- [ ] RC transmitter operational (safety override)
- [ ] Telemetry link active
- [ ] Geofence and RTL configured

## Path to BVLOS (Future)

When you need beyond visual line of sight:
1. Study UK SORA (Specific Operations Risk Assessment)
2. Obtain Remote Pilot Certificate Level 2 (RPC-L2)
3. Apply for Operational Authorisation
4. Budget: £2,185+ and several weeks
5. Consider starting with PDRA01 (£500/yr, 24-hour turnaround) for enhanced VLOS
