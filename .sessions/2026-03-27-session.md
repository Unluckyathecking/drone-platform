## Decisions Made

- **All 46 audit issues approved** — every FAIL and CAUTION reviewed individually, fixes agreed
- **Canada replaces India** for international operations — Mohammed has dual UK/Canadian citizenship, Canada has routine BVLOS since Nov 2025
- **C2 pivot approved as strategic direction** — build mission engine as C2 platform from day one, monetise via DaaS first
- **Airship wind turbines** — 5-15kW from frame-mounted turbines fills the night power gap
- **Laser systems moved to airship only** — balloon gets 1-2 × 500W max, airship's 5,000kg payload budget absorbs 200kg of lasers easily
- **Bungee launcher primary** for MINI tier — pneumatic catapult deferred to funded phase (pressure vessel certification required)
- **In-place charging primary** — battery swap deferred to purpose-built fixtures with funding
- **45° dovetail** replacing 60° — easier to print, equally strong (Arca-Swiss proven)
- **0.55mm clearance** replacing 0.3mm — accounts for FDM tolerance and thermal expansion
- **4-pole Anderson** replacing 2-pole — simultaneous 5V + 12V + VBATT access
- **Threaded rod battery rail** replacing Velcro — positive-lock CG management
- **M4 through-bolt lanyard** replacing bare PETG hole — won't shear under dynamic load
- **LARGE tier redesigned** — 10-12m wingspan, 150-200kg MTOW, L/D 26-32, custom endurance-first airframe
- **Solar reclassified** — perpetual flight achievable on custom LARGE in summer, not on multipurpose small airframe
- **Open source = credibility strategy**, not revenue model
- **DaaS margins corrected** to 35-50% (not 80-90%)
- **3 customer conversations** added to Phase 0 checklist
- **School political connections noted** as long-term strategic asset
